My impromptu plan to pursue MS

So it was the peak COVID time in 2019 and it was already one year since I completed my undergrad. I was sitting with my family discussing various career options with my mom and dad. It had always been my mother's dream that someday I go to the US to pursue a Master's and land a job there, and so my mom all of a sudden raised this topic in front of my dad. My dad wasn't so convinced about this decision. Although I wasn't against this idea, so I agreed with my mom. It took a little effort to persuade my dad for this but we were able to do so in the end.

Within one month, I prepared for the GRE, took the GRE, and could apply to a total of only 3 universities, since the application deadlines for most of the universities were already over. Some universities including Stevens extended their deadlines due to the pandemic. I got a call from Stevens, got my visa, and boom.....I was in the US within one month of my decision.
